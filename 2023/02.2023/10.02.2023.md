## Situation

Italy and France are ready to transfer SAMP-T air defense to Ukraine. This will be announced "in the coming days" - Italian Prime Minister Meloni

As spring approaches, Russia's invasion of Ukraine is entering a new phase. To launch successful counteroffensives, Ukrainian forces have to punch through layers of Russian defenses. 

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/34960418/218200500-888fe779-e9d5-4eaf-bcd1-7a732cb9a241.png)

The first week of February saw the Russian Winter Offensive move into full swing as major pushes were made in Kreminna, Bakhmut, and Vuhledar. The Russians made some gains but Ukrainian defenses held.
